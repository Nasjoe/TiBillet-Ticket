Branding

Is your brand driving your business forwards? We’re a leading UK branding agency with a proven record in creating iconic brands that improve performance across every metric.

Brand Strategy

Is your brand delivering growth? It will when driven by sharp, action-oriented strategy. Our brand strategists help fuel the growth of some of the fastest growing companies in the UK and Europe. We will help you create a brand strategy that elevates your business to new heights.

Creating New Brands

How can you create a new brand that is loved, even before it is launched? We’ve successfully created and delivered over 100 brands from start-ups, to scale-ups and brands for adidas, the BBC, and Cancer Research. How? Through CoCreation, a relentless focus on process, and a talented, highly experienced team of branding specialists.

Evolving Existing Brands

How relevant is your brand? Great brands are always in beta, shaping attitudes and markets, but also reacting to them. Our proprietary analytics and audit tools will show you when and how to evolve. We’ve successfully achieved this with energetic scale-ups like LAD Bible through to cherished, 150yr old professional services firms.

Brand Review / Audit

Where is the untapped potential for your business and brand? We’ve built a digital brand audit tool based on our experience in consulting for some of the world’s leading firms, including EY, the BBC and adidas. We’ll show you exactly where your potential lies, and how to harness it.

Brand Guidelines & Governance

How clear is your identity? How consistent is your brand? Clearly defining everything you stand for is crucial for your brand to deliver its potential. We create detailed brand guidelines and govern them for clients, to ensure the brand maximises its potential.

Employer Branding

Do your people know exactly who you are, what you do, and how and why you do it? Equally do they know how they can be successful in your company? Attracting and retaining talented people will fuel your business’ growth. And your brand is the unifying force that will attract, retain, unite and inspire your people.

BBC x Bert. Name & Brand ID

The BBC appointed us as its branding agency for the launch of its new online gaming platform and division.

Our naming team worked with the BBC’s in-house gaming team to create ‘Open Games’. We created a narrative that simplified the premise, explaining that Open Games connects the world’s leading games developers with some of the best loved media brands on the planet.

CoCreating from Media City, the narrative was brought to life in rich digital form. The animated logo, embodying the Open Games portal, creates a unique way for multiple brands to share the limelight on communications. The ‘O’ dynamically wraps around the content within, resulting in a new brand capable of pairing with globally recognised media brands including Match of the Day, Top Gear and The Octonauts.

Karl Hughes, BBC Open Games Programme Lead, said “Of all the partners we’ve worked with since moving to Manchester, Bert’s process and the resulting brand places them right at the top.”

Federal x Bert. Brand research, strategy & brand ID

With a new opening on the horizon, Federal approached us to help understand and amplify the magic of its original Northern Quarter store.

Always prepared to cover the hard yards, our strategy team had the unenviable task of consuming plates of halloumi & ‘shrooms and cheddar corn fritters (washed down with bottomless long-blacks) whilst chatting with Federal clientele.

The caffeine and warmth of the welcome helped us quickly uncover Federal’s formula. We then created a road-map and refreshed identity that’s taken a Manchester staple to even greater heights.

Put simply, Manchester isn’t Manchester without a morning trip to Federal. It’s the mecca of brunch spots, now attracting 40k Insta followers and queues of upto an hour (even on the drizzliest of days).

Cancer Research x Bert. Strategy, name and visual ID.

We won the competitive pitch to become Cancer Research UK’s branding agency for a campaign aimed at accelerating the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of cancer globally.

CoCreation helped align stakeholders from multiple organisations, building a name, narrative and visual identity together. Our Re-Write Cancer campaign aims to alter cancer’s story, replacing uncertainty and fear with the confidence that a future free of cancer can be achieved for all within decades, if leading partners collaborate and concentrate resources effectively.
